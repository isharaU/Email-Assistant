Privacy Policy

Sinhala Email Assistant
Effective Date: September 15, 2025

Data We Collect

Personal communications (email content): The extension reads the text of your draft or received email to generate a reply.
Website content (Gmail compose window): The extension interacts with Gmail's UI to insert the AI-generated reply.

How We Use Data

Email content is sent securely to our backend API only for the purpose of generating a reply.
Data is processed in real time and not stored permanently on our servers.
No data is used for advertising, analytics, or tracking.
Real-time Processing
No Permanent Storage

What We Don't Collect

We respect your privacy completely. We do NOT collect:

No personally identifiable information (name, address, payment details)
No health, financial, authentication, or location data
No browsing history or user activity outside Gmail

Data Sharing

Zero Data Sharing Policy:

We do not sell, share, or transfer user data to third parties.
Data is only used to provide the single function of generating AI replies.
No Third Parties
Single Purpose Use
